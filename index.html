About Us

About Pets Avenue Animal Hospital!

"Your Pets' Ultimate Care Hub"

At Pets Avenue, we understand that your furry friends are more than just pets – they are cherished members of your family. As Abu Dhabi’s premier veterinary hospital, we are committed to providing top-notch care and compassion to every animal we treat. With our experienced veterinarians and dedicated staff, we offer a wide range of services from routine check-ups to advanced diagnostics and surgical procedures.

What sets us apart is our state-of-the-art MRI facility, allowing us to provide accurate and timely diagnoses for your pets. With our cutting-edge technology, we can uncover hidden health issues and tailor treatment plans specifically to meet your pet’s needs.
